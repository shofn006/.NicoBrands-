Nico Brands LLC

Nico Brands is a mastermind brand collective dedicated to identifying gaps in the everyday products, wants, and needs that shape how we live. Each brand under the Nico umbrella is a passionately built project—designed to elevate the customer experience through better quality, more thoughtful design, or accessible pricing.

From solving frustrations to reimagining everyday essentials, Nico Brands exists to make life just a little better, one product at a time.

Currently, Nico Brands consists of three flagship brands:

  • Montessori Mama — Wooden modern Montessori-inspired toys designed for hands-on learning and lasting play.
  • Dinoland — A bold, dinosaur-themed brand with high-quality designs that fuel imagination, exploration, and fun.
  • Pure Japan — Premium Japanese matcha and green tea sourced directly from farms and crafted for authenticity, flavor, and transparency.
